Primary Research System

Von Mises Bistable UAV Wing Research

Experimental bistable wing platform studying dynamic snap-through thresholds, rotating actuation, nonlinear structural response, embedded instrumentation, and future UAV implementation. The system integrates NEMA 17 and NEMA 23 stepper systems, A4988 and DM542 drivers, Arduino Mega control architecture, ICM-20948 and ICM-42688 IMUs, OpenCV tracking, MATLAB analysis pipelines, and experimental signal acquisition workflows.

  • Rotating arm snap-through threshold testing
  • Centrifugally assisted Von Mises truss experiments
  • Serial logging and event detection workflows
  • OpenCV marker tracking and FFT analysis
